Cricket Rules

General Rules:

Team :
A cricket match is played between two teams, each consisting of 11 players.
Over:
An over consists of 6 legal deliveries bowled by a bowler.
Umpire:
Two on-field umpires officiate the game, with the option of a third umpire for TV replays.

Additional Rules:

Extra Time :
In knockout matches, two 15-minute halves may be played if scores are tied after 90 minutes.
Added Time:
The referee may add extra minutes to compensate for stoppages during the game. Let me know if you want details on any specific rule
