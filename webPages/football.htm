FOOTBALL

FootBall Rules

General Rules:

Team :
Each team consists of 11 players on the field, including a goalkeeper. A team must have at least 7 players to start or continue a match.
Time:
A match lasts for 90 minutes, divided into two halves of 45 minutes each.
Goal:
A goal is scored when the ball completely crosses the goal line between the goalposts and under the crossbar.
