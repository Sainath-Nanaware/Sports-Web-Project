KABADDI

Kabaddi Rules

General Rules:

Duration of the Game :
A match consists of two halves of 20 minutes each, with a 5-minute break at halftime. Teams switch sides after halftime.
Court Dimensions:
The playing area is a rectangular court measuring 13m x 10m for men and 12m x 8m for women.
